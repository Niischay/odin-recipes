Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Description

Alfredo Pasta is a luscious dish featuring fettuccine coated in a velvety sauce of butter, cream, and Parmesan. This classic Italian comfort food promises a rich, satisfying dining experience.

Ingredients
  • 250 g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: minced garlic, nutmeg, parsley for flavour
Steps
  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan,melt the butter over medium heat. If using garlic, saute it until fragment.
  3. Pour in the hravy cream and bring it to a simmer. Stir continuously.
  4. Add the Parmesan cheese gradually, stirring until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste, Add a pinch of nutmeg for extra flavour if desired.
  6. Toss the cooked pasta into the Alfredo sauce, costing it evenly.
  7. Garnish with parsely and more Parmesan if you like.
  8. Serve immmediately and enjoy your delicious Alfredo Pasta!
