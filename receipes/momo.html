Momo Recipe

Description

Momo, a delectable Tibetan dumpling, features minced meat or vegetables encased in thin dough, steamed or fried. Served with flavorful dipping sauces, momos are a cherished culinary delight worldwide.

Ingredients
  1. Dough
    • All-purpose flour
    • Water
    • Salt
  2. Filling
    • Minced Meat(chicken,pork or beef) or Vegetables
    • Onions
    • Garlic
    • Soy Sauce
    • Oil
    • Spices(like cumin, coriander and chilli powder)
  3. Seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper
  4. Optional
    • Cilantro
Steps
  1. In a bowl, mix the ground meat, chopped vegetables, garlic paste, ginger paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Take a momo wrapper and place a spoonful of the meat mixture in the center.
  3. Fold the wrapper in half, creating a half-moon shape.
  4. Seal the edges by pinching and folding, creating pleats for a traditional look.
  5. Repeat the process until all the mixture is used.
  6. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes or until cooked thoroughly.
  7. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
