Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish featuring tender chicken pieces in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. Infused with aromatic spices, it's a flavorful and indulgent culinary experience.

Ingredients
  • CHicken(boneless)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Red chilli powder
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt
  • Oil
Steps
  1. Marinate chicken pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook marinated chicken until browned. Set aside.
  3. In another pan, melt butter. Add cumin, coriander, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree, garam masala, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add cooked chicken and cream. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Crush kasuri methi and sprinkle over the dish.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
